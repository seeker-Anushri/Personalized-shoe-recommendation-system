Medical Solutions

Solutions for Flatfoot

  • Custom orthotic insoles to provide better arch support.
  • Physical therapy exercises to strengthen the foot muscles:

    1. Heel cord stretches: Lengthen the Achilles tendon to improve flexibility.
    2. Arch lifts: Strengthen the intrinsic muscles supporting the arch.
    3. Towel curls: Use your toes to scrunch a towel toward you to build foot strength.
    4. Ball rolls: Massage and stretch the plantar fascia.
    5. Toe raises: Enhance ankle stability and shin strength.

  • Supportive footwear with cushioning and arch support.
  • Surgical options for severe cases.

When to Seek Medical Assistance

  • Persistent pain, stiffness, or difficulty walking.
  • Symptoms despite non-surgical treatments.
  • History of foot injuries or deformities.

Solutions for High Arch

  • Cushioning insoles to reduce foot strain.
  • Stretching exercises to relieve tight muscles.
  • Shoes with proper arch support.
  • Physical therapy to improve balance and function.

Recommended Exercises

  1. Balance Exercises: Improve proprioception using wobble boards or one-leg stands.
  2. Ankle Strengthening: Inversion and eversion exercises for stability.
  3. Arch Lifts: Build strength in muscles supporting the arch.
  4. Heel Cord Stretch: Reduce calf tension and plantar pressure.

Solutions for Normal Foot

  • Maintain healthy weight to prevent pressure build-up.
  • Regular stretching and strengthening of foot muscles.
  • Wear supportive shoes that match activity type.
  • Get routine check-ups with a podiatrist.
